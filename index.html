About the tool

Early-stage support for clearer renovation choices before detailed design and major expenditure.

What the tool is

This early-stage decision-support tool helps homeowners, housing managers, condominium representatives, renovation advisors, designers, construction professionals and public support organisations explore which Deep Energy Renovation pathways may be feasible for a specific multifamily building situation.

How it helps

The tool compares possible renovation pathways using building context, budget capacity, selected measures, quantities, user priorities, coordination difficulty, disruption and implementation readiness.

Why early-stage decisions matter

Buildings are central to Europe’s climate and energy transition. Many renovation projects slow down because priorities, finance, ownership coordination, resident disruption and first-step decisions are not clear enough at the beginning. The tool supports users before detailed design, technical studies or expensive renovation works begin.
